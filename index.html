About Me

I hold a B.Sc. in Biology and Earth Science from the Hebrew University, where I developed a strong interest in neurobiology, physiology, atmospheric physics, and climate science.

I began coding during my academic studies and later taught myself frontend development. I’ve since built and contributed to real-world web applications using modern tools and frameworks.

I recently completed a 10-month software engineering program at Masterschool, with a focus on backend and full-stack development using Python, Flask, SQL, Node.js, and REST APIs.

Currently, I'm a frontend developer at Mobiliter, where I design and implement UI components with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and WordPress. I also create visual assets and videos using tools like Photoshop and Premiere, and use generative AI to enhance creative workflows.

My background in video editing from my army service sharpened my visual storytelling and teamwork skills—something I continue to apply in my creative tech work.
